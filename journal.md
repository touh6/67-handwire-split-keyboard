i opened the google doc i made and figured out the exact components i require for my 67 keyboard 

i opened up solidworks and i really struggled with making the initial case for 6 as the upper half of 6 was difficult to make because of it's structure 

these are the components i am using:
key switches (mx style) = cherry mx
diodes (1N4148) = to avoid ghosting 
Microcontroller = arduino pro micro x 2 (master and slave)
TRRS jacks for split communication 
heat inserts
m3 screws
22-24 AWG solid copper wire for matrix 

and the more i kept researching, the more i realized was that this is not the most practical project and i'm just doing it for the love of the game, it is barely going to be useful 

and i was done with the 6 case by the end of the recording
